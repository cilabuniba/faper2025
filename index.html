ADV 2025 - International Workshop on Advances in Drone Vision

In conjunction with ICIAP 2025 | September 15 or 16, 2025

About the Workshop

The International Workshop on Advances in Drone Vision (ADV 2025) aims to bring together top experts from academia and industry to explore the latest research in drone-based computer vision technologies. Drones are now vital across numerous sectors, including agriculture, logistics, infrastructure monitoring, disaster response, and environmental surveillance.

As drone autonomy evolves, computer vision is becoming the essential intelligence behind their capabilities. From object detection and scene understanding to real-time edge computing, this workshop focuses on making aerial robotics smarter, faster, and more reliable. ADV 2025 will showcase vision algorithms for onboard processing, anomaly detection, aerial image segmentation, and much more.

Our goal is to foster interdisciplinary collaboration, discuss breakthrough methodologies, and highlight the critical role of vision in next-gen aerial systems.

Topics of Interest

  • Object detection & tracking in aerial imagery
  • Aerial image segmentation
  • Autonomous navigation and path planning
  • Energy-efficient vision & edge computing
  • Anomaly detection in aerial footage
  • Real-time drone decision-making
